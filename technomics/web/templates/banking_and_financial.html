Technomics offers a next generation global Enterprise Banking Platform enabling banks to achieve a consolidated view of any customer across all service delivery channels, thus extending their customer reach. The available functionality satisfies the requirements of both, Retail and Corporate banking.

    Features
  • Extensively parameterized
  • User definable validations
  • Secure through maker-checker concept
  • User definable charges
  • Multi language support
  • Service branch
  • Over 1500 built in reports
  • Interfaces to various channels
  • Built in report writer for others
    Browser Based
  • Technomics is cross browser compatible application.
    Open Platform
  • It can be implemented on a wide range of hardware and software platforms – leaving you free to choose them depending on the exact requirements and situation. Choose from Windows, Linux, and various versions of Intel-UNIX and various versions of RISC-UNIX.
    Standards Compliant
  • Technomics complies with ISO 8583 standards.
    Highly Scalable
  • Technomics uses component based, J2EE framework for development. Technomics is entirely a Stateless Application. Among others, this framework provides a high degree of scalability.
 
    Web Service Enabled
  • Technomics is Web service enabled and allows integration with other applications.
    Functionality
  • Demand Deposits: Savings, Current, etc.
  • Time Deposits: Simple, Cumulative, Recurring, Flexi, etc. MAS / AIMAS
  • Demand Loans: Over Draft, Cash Credit, Hypothecations, pledge, supply bills, packing credits, etc.
  • Term Loans: Short, Long, Medium Term Loans, Mortgages, etc.
  • General Ledger: Back Office, Trial Balance, Balance Sheet, Profit/Loss
  • Bills: OBC/IBC, BP, BD, FOBC, FIBC, FBP, FBN
  • Bank Guarantees: Inland, Foreign, Performance, Financial
  • Letters of Credits: Inland, Foreign, Irrevocable, Back to Back
  • Transactions: Cash Deposits and Payments, Clearing Inward and Outward, Transfers between Accounts,
  • Interest: Calculation and Application
  • Various Charges: Calculation and Application Exchange of Currencies NPA Management
    Services
  • Personal information, Signature/Photograph Capture/Retrieval, Memo Creation for an Account and display Safe Deposit Lockers, Remittances (DD, PO, MT, TT etc.) Funds Transfer, Standing Instructions, Other Foreign Exchange Activities 
    Interfaces
  • Standard 8583 interface, ATM transactions, POS, Debit Cards, Mobile Banking, Tele Banking, SMS, Internet Access, Payment Gateway Interface (Utility Bill Payment etc) Interface to pigmy devices 
    Reports & Queries
  • Statutory, Mandatory, As and When, Over 400 standard reports, Oracle and Crystal can be used in addition 
    Flexible Financial Channel Integration
  • Technomics connects to various financial systems and devices through an open delivery channel controller. The controller is a network enabled component which listens to various devices through an standard message format like ISO8583 and XML.
  • Open channel allows integration with channels such as ATM, POS devices, Payment gateways IVRs, internet, mobile.
